### Vision and mission
The MLNLP community is committed to enhancing communication and collaboration between academics, industry professionals, and enthusiasts in the fields of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML), both domestically and internationally. Our core mission is to advance the entire field through open dialogue and sharing, with a special emphasis on supporting and guiding beginners and students. In terms of community activities and resources, we organize both online and offline technical exchange meetings, inviting experts and pioneers from the field to share the latest research results and industry trends. Additionally, we maintain and share a variety of NLP and ML open-source projects on GitHub, aiding community members in easily accessing real-world projects and the latest technologies. We also offer tutorials, workshops, and training courses for beginners and students to help them build a solid foundation of knowledge and stay abreast of the latest advancements in the field. Furthermore, we are dedicated to fostering collaboration between academia and industry by sharing datasets, research tools, and cooperative projects, thus promoting the integration of laboratory research with practical applications.
